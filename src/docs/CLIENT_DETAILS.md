## 🔧 Business Processes to Digitize

### **Sales Process**

**Current (Manual):**
1. Customer walks in
2. Browse physical stock
3. Ask questions (owner answers)
4. Purchase decision
5. Manual receipt
6. Cash/card payment
7. Take product home

**New (Digital + Physical):**
1. Customer browses online OR walks in
2. Add to cart online OR in-store
3. Checkout (online payment OR in-store)
4. Order confirmation (email)
5. Pick up OR delivery
6. Digital receipt
7. Purchase history saved
8. Follow-up email

### **Service Booking Process**

**Current (Manual):**
1. Customer calls shop
2. Owner checks paper calendar
3. Agree on time (phone)
4. Owner writes in book
5. Customer must remember
6. Service performed
7. Manual invoice

**New (Digital):**
1. Customer books online (24/7)
2. System checks availability
3. Instant confirmation email
4. Automated reminders (SMS/email)
5. Service performed
6. Digital invoice
7. Service history saved
8. Follow-up & feedback request

### **Inventory Management**

**Current (Manual):**
1. Sell product
2. Owner marks in notebook
3. Weekly stock count
4. Notice low stock
5. Call supplier
6. Receive delivery
7. Update notebook

**New (Digital):**
1. Sell product (online/in-store)
2. System auto-decrements stock
3. Real-time stock visibility
4. Low stock alerts (automatic)
5. One-click reorder
6. Receive delivery
7. Scan barcode → stock updated
8. Analytics show trends

## 🚧 Migration Challenges

### **Data Migration**
**Challenge:** Convert Excel/paper records to database  
**Items to Migrate:**
- Product catalog (~200-500 items)
- Customer contact info (~1,000 contacts)
- Historical sales (1-2 years)
- Service records
- Inventory levels

**Strategy:**
- Start with new system (clean slate)
- Migrate critical data manually
- Accept some historical data loss
- Focus on future data quality

### **Owner Training**
**Challenge:** Low-medium tech literacy  
**Strategy:**
- Simple, intuitive interface
- Step-by-step video tutorials
- Phone/email support
- Gradual rollout
- Parallel systems initially (Excel + new system)

### **Customer Adoption**
**Challenge:** Existing customers used to in-store only  
**Strategy:**
- Promote website in-store
- Offer online-exclusive deals
- Email marketing to existing base
- QR codes in shop
- Maintain excellent in-store experience
